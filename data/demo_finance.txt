Title: Emergency Fund Basics (Source: DemoKnowledgeBase)
An emergency fund helps a small organization handle unexpected expenses without relying on expensive debt. A practical starting point is to set aside a small percentage of weekly revenue until the fund can cover several weeks of essential costs.

Title: Understanding Credit (Source: DemoKnowledgeBase)
Credit can support growth when repayment terms, interest rates, and cash flow impact are understood before borrowing. A good repayment history is built by paying obligations on time and avoiding informal loans with unclear conditions.
