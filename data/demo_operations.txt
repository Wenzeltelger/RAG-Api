Title: Supplier Payment Workflow (Source: DemoKnowledgeBase)
To record a supplier payment in a demo operations workflow, open the application, choose the supplier payment option, enter the invoice reference, verify the amount, and confirm the transaction. The payment status should be reviewed after submission.

Title: Inventory Management (Source: DemoKnowledgeBase)
Healthy inventory practices reduce missed sales and waste. Review high-turnover products frequently, organize stock using first-in-first-out handling, and schedule periodic physical inventory counts to keep records aligned with available items.
